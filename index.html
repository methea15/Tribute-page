Norodom Sihanouk

The King Father of Cambodia

Timeline of the Late King Father

  • 1922 --born in Phnom Penh, Cambodia
  • 1936 --under his maternal grandparent care, he attend his education at a boarding school.
  • 1941 --First coronation after reigning king died
  • 1945 --under pressure from Japanese, Sihanouk serve as a king and prime minister at the same time.
  • 1947 --introduce first election and freedom of media to Cambodia
  • 1949 --negotiate to be independent from French government
  • 9 November 1953 --declaration of independent from France

    • The First Kingdom of Cambodia, commonly as Sangkum era

  • 1955 --abdicated the throne to pursue politic, formation of his own political party "the Sangkum" which later replacing democrats in National Assembly.
  • 1955-1960 --resign and retook post of prime minisiter citing fatigue.
  • 1960 --appointed Head of State which equivalent to power of the king.
  • 1967 --attempt to reconciliation with the US and to get viet cong troop leaving Cambodia
  • 1970 --formation of resistance movement FUNK to join him and fight aagainst Lon Nol's government. His endorsement of Khmer Rouge whom convey his message to his people and rooting him for his cause.

    • End of the era

  • 1975 --Democratic Kampuchea formmed with Sihanouk as its head of state. After return from lobby for diplomatic recognition, he resigned when he saw th use of forced labour by khmer rouge government that later put him and royal family under house arrest at the royal palace.
  • 1985 --he appointed his son to lead ANS
  • 1992 --left to seek medical treatment in Beijing
  • 1993 --Sihanouk appoint Ranariddh and Hun Sen as co-prime ministers with equal powers. Sihanouk also reinstated as th King of Cambodia.
  • 1997 --clashed between CPP and FUNCINPEC, power struggle between two prime minister.
  • 2004 --he publicly advocated and back same-sex marriage and engaged in humanitarian works and projects. In later month, he abdicated the throne and throne council unanimously voted to select Norodom Sihamoni as th next king.
  • 2012 --died of a heart attack at the age of 89
